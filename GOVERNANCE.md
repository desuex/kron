# Governance

Kron is currently maintained by its original author.

---

## Project Model

- Benevolent maintainer model.
- Decisions prioritize correctness and long-term stability.
- Backward compatibility is taken seriously.

---

## Decision Making

- Major design decisions are discussed via issues.
- Behavior changes require justification and documentation updates.
- Breaking changes require version bump.

---

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:

- Reviewing pull requests
- Maintaining deterministic guarantees
- Protecting core invariants
- Ensuring security and correctness

---

## Future Evolution

Governance may evolve if:

- Multiple long-term contributors emerge
- The project grows significantly
- A foundation adoption is considered

Until then, stability and consistency are prioritized.
